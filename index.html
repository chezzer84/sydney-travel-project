Why Sydney?

Sydney, a city steeped in history and alive with modern culture.

Nestled along the stunning shores of Australia's east coast, this iconic metropolis boasts a rich tapestry of heritage, from its indigenous roots to its colonial past. Wander through the cobbled streets of The Rocks, where echoes of convict history mingle with the buzz of contemporary cafes and galleries. Marvel at architectural wonders like the Sydney Opera House, a symbol of innovation and artistic brilliance.

But Sydney isn't just about its past; it's a dynamic hub of creativity and innovation. Dive into its thriving arts scene, where world-class museums, theaters, and street performances captivate audiences. Indulge in a culinary adventure, savoring flavors from around the globe in bustling markets and Michelin-starred restaurants. With its stunning beaches, bustling harbor, and welcoming spirit, Sydney offers an unparalleled quality of life, making it not just a city, but an experience like no other."

Cocktail Bars

My favourite cocktail bars in Sydney

Little Darlin

Convivial locale serving inventive cocktails and wooden boards topped with gourmet pizzas and tapas.

Address

420 Elizabeth St, Surry Hills NSW 2010

What I like about it

Great happy hour prices on cocktails and yummy pizzas. The cocktails often come with sweets and lollys and are all delicious. The decor is girly and fun, and location is close to central station.

Saga Bar

SAGA Bar is a mythical oasis blending Indian, Middle Eastern, and Southeast Asian influences, offering exclusive cocktails, and perfectly paired food in an unforgettable experience.

Address

49-51 Goold Street Chippendale 2008

What I like about it

It feels like another world hidden behind 2 wooden doors. The tapas style food and unusual cocktail collection makes this a favourite hideaway!

Opera Bar

Opera Bar is the most iconic bar in Sydney with a view of Harbour Bridge and Opera House, offering beer, wine and food for different cravings and tastes.

Address

Sydney Opera House, Lower Concourse Level, Sydney NSW 2000

What I like about it

So this bar is all about the location and the hustle and bustle, the cocktails and food may be more mainstream but you come for the vibes!
